In case you & your housemates are unable to connect to the Wi-Fi connection, please file a complaint to your internet Service Provider(TIME)

  2. Step 2 : Please Provide the following details:
    • Service Number:
    • Unit Number:
    • Company Name:
  3. Step 3 : Explain the issue you are facing
  4. Step 4 : Make an appointment with TIME Technical team during the phone call/conversation
  5. Step 5 : Provide your contact details (Name & Phone Number) for the appointment
  6. Step 6 : Please ensure to request for the reference/case number, to APU technical/Warden staff can follow-up on the case.
  7. Step 7 : Please ensure to SMS/WhatsApp/Call the warden and give the 'case/reference' number for a follow-up
