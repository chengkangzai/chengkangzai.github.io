What to do when you locked the projector?

Follow This Step

  1. Leave the projector powered on until it goes in to Standby Mode (approx. 5 minutes) The screen will go black once its on standby mode.
  2. Unplug or poweroff the projector and wait 20 seconds
  3. Plug the projector back in.
  4. Power the projector on.
  5. You should be prompted for a password.
  6. Contact Hardware member to unlock the projector
