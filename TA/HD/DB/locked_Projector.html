What to do when you locked the projector?

Follow This Step

  1. Leave the projector powered on until it goes in to Standby Mode (approx. 5 minutes) The screen will go black and the power indicator light will turn orange.
  2. Unplug the projector (you may need a ladder) and wait 20 seconds
  3. Plug the projector back in.
  4. Power the projector on.
  5. You should be prompted for a password.
  6. Enter "0000" by pressing the "Freeze" key on the remote four times.
  7. You projector should be unlocked.
